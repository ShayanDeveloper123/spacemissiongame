About

Shayan Game is designed to challenge players with a variety of obstacles and puzzles. As you explore the alien spaceship, you'll encounter different levels with increasing difficulty. The game includes features such as laser obstacles, white balls, and linear balls, each adding to the complexity of the game. Your mission is to navigate through these challenges, unlocking doors, avoiding hazards, and completing levels. The game also features high-quality graphics and an engaging storyline, making it a must-play for adventure enthusiasts.
